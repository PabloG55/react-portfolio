Skipper – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2025

Skipper respects your privacy. Below we explain what data we collect and how it’s used.

Accessibility Services

Skipper uses Android’s Accessibility Service strictly to help users with motor or visual impairments automatically press “Skip Ad” buttons in supported apps (like YouTube). This functionality is completely local and runs on your device.

Advertising

Skipper uses Google AdMob to display ads, including rewarded ads. AdMob may collect certain information such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device and usage data
  • IP address

This information is used for ad personalization and analytics. You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings.

Learn more: Google Ad Technologies

Data Collection

Apart from data collected by AdMob, Skipper itself does not collect or store any user data.

Third-Party Access

AdMob (Google LLC) is the only third party that receives data for the purpose of serving ads.

Your Choices

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
