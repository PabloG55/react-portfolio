Skipper – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

Skipper respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

Accessibility Services

Skipper uses Android’s Accessibility Service strictly to help users with motor or visual impairments automatically press “Skip Ad” buttons in supported apps (like YouTube). This functionality is completely local and runs on your device.

Data Collection

Skipper does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal data
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers

Third-Party Access

No data is shared with any third party. The app functions independently without network communication.
